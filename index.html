FizzBuzz

FizzBuzz is a great brainteaser to keep coders on their toes. It is a poplular algorithm for coders to write a program that prints the numbers 1-100 while printing "Fizz" for multiples of a given number, "Buzz" for multiples of another given number and "FizzBuzz" for multiples of both of those.
